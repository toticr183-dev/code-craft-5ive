About Code Craft 5ive

Five young Ethiopians on a mission to transform the digital landscape, one project at a time.

Our Story

Code Craft 5ive was born from a simple truth: great things happen when hungry minds come together. We are five friends from Ethiopia who refused to wait for opportunities - we decided to create them.

Starting with just HTML and CSS, Tofik built over 12 websites before even owning a phone. That fire inspired the others. Fenet picked up video editing. Lema mastered design. Melat learned to build audiences. Beza became the glue that holds us together.

Today, we are not just a team. We are a movement. We believe Ethiopian creators can compete with anyone in the world. And we're proving it, one project at a time.

Our Mission

To empower Ethiopian businesses and creators with world-class digital solutions, proving that talent knows no borders. We exist to bridge the gap between local creativity and global standards.

Our Vision

A future where "Made in Ethiopia" is synonymous with digital excellence. We envision a generation of Ethiopian creators leading the African tech revolution.
